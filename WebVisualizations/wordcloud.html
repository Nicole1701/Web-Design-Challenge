“Identity theft is not a joke, Jim! Millions of families suffer every year.” – Dwight Schrute

"Well", "right" and "think" are the most said words across the entire series.

After removing punctuation and the most commonly used words in the English language (e.g., "I", "a", "the"), a word cloud can be created to find the most used words used throughout the series. It can help find patterns and see if any words are unique to certain characters.

The bigger the word, the more often it is repeated in the dialogue.
